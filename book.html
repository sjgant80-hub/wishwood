Common questions

Before you book.

Is it really off-grid? How does that work day-to-day?

Solar powers low-draw lights. Wood-burner heats the cabin and the water (with the gas backup for the shower). Composting loos. Gas hob in the outdoor kitchen for cooking. You're not roughing it — you've just stepped out of the grid for a few days.

Can we bring our dog(s)?

Yes — all three cabins are dog-friendly. £15 per dog flat for the whole stay. Outdoor warm-water rinse by the boiler shed. 16 acres to roam. Just keep them on the lead near the boundary.

Wifi? Phone signal?

No wifi by design. Phone signal is patchy (decent for EE, less for O2). If you genuinely need to work remotely, this isn't the spot. If you want to be unreachable for 48 hours, it's perfect.

What's included? What should we bring?

We provide: bedding, towels, kitchen kit, gas cylinder, one log net, kindling and firelighters, candles, basic cleaning kit. Bring: warm clothes (even in summer evenings), torches, food, drinks. Extra log nets £5 if you go through one.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 14 days before check-in. Inside 14 days: 50% refund + we try to re-let; if we re-let, full refund. Life happens — message us.

Where exactly is it?

CT3 4NG, between Calcott, Sturry and Broad Oak, adjacent to Blean Woods National Nature Reserve. Canterbury 20 min, Whitstable 20 min, Herne Bay 10 min. Full directions sent T-1 day before arrival.
